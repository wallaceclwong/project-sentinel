# REGULATORY COMPLIANCE GUIDE
## Project Sentinel - Professional Trading Requirements

### 🏛️ REGULATORY OVERVIEW

**US Regulatory Bodies:**
- **CFTC** (Commodity Futures Trading Commission): Futures market oversight
- **NFA** (National Futures Association): Industry self-regulation
- **SEC** (Securities and Exchange Commission): Securities oversight
- **FINRA** (Financial Industry Regulatory Authority): Brokerage oversight

**Key Regulations:**
- **Commodity Exchange Act**: Futures trading regulations
- **Dodd-Frank Act**: Market reforms and protections
- **Anti-Money Laundering (AML)**: Transaction monitoring
- **Know Your Customer (KYC)**: Customer verification

### 📋 REQUIRED REGISTRATIONS

**NFA Registration (Required for Futures Trading):**
- **Category**: Commodity Trading Advisor (CTA) or Commodity Pool Operator (CPO)
- **Requirements**: 
  - Series 3 exam (or exemption)
  - Background check
  - Net worth requirements
  - Compliance program
- **Cost**: $200-500 registration fee + annual dues
- **Timeline**: 4-8 weeks for approval

**Brokerage Registration:**
- **Account Type**: Professional/Institutional
- **Requirements**: 
  - Accredited investor status
  - Professional trader status
  - Net worth verification
  - Experience documentation
- **Cost**: Varies by brokerage
- **Timeline**: 2-4 weeks for approval

### 💼 PROFESSIONAL STATUS REQUIREMENTS

**Accredited Investor Definition:**
- **Net Worth**: $1,000,000+ (excluding primary residence)
- **Income**: $200,000+ (individual) or $300,000+ (joint) for last 2 years
- **Professional Knowledge**: Demonstrated trading experience
- **Risk Tolerance**: Understanding of futures risks

**Professional Trader Status:**
- **Experience**: 2+ years of active trading
- **Volume**: High trading frequency
- **Knowledge**: Understanding of derivatives markets
- **Capital**: Significant personal capital at risk

### 📊 COMPLIANCE REQUIREMENTS

**Anti-Money Laundering (AML):**
- **Customer Identification Program (CIP)**: Verify customer identity
- **Transaction Monitoring**: Detect suspicious activity
- **Reporting Requirements**: File SARs for suspicious transactions
- **Record Keeping**: 5 years of transaction records

**Know Your Customer (KYC):**
- **Identity Verification**: Government-issued ID
- **Address Verification**: Utility bills, bank statements
- **Source of Funds**: Documentation of capital sources
- **Risk Assessment**: Customer risk profiling

**Trading Compliance:**
- **Position Limits**: Maximum contract positions
- **Reporting Requirements**: Large position reporting
- **Trade Surveillance**: Monitor for manipulation
- **Best Execution**: Fair order execution

### 📋 DOCUMENTATION REQUIREMENTS

**Personal Documentation:**
- **Government ID**: Passport, driver's license
- **Proof of Address**: Utility bills, bank statements
- **Social Security Number**: For tax reporting
- **Professional References**: Trading experience verification

**Financial Documentation:**
- **Bank Statements**: Last 3-6 months
- **Brokerage Statements**: Last 6-12 months
- **Tax Returns**: Last 2-3 years
- **Net Worth Statement**: Detailed asset/liability summary
- **Source of Funds**: Capital origin documentation

**Business Documentation:**
- **Business Entity**: LLC, corporation, or partnership
- **Operating Agreement**: Business structure documents
- **EIN Number**: Employer identification number
- **Business Bank Account**: Separate from personal accounts
- **Professional Insurance**: Liability coverage
