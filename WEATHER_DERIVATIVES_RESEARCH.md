# WEATHER DERIVATIVES MARKET RESEARCH
## Project Sentinel - Real Trading Preparation

### 📊 MARKET OVERVIEW

**Weather Derivatives Market:**
- **Primary Exchanges**: CME Group, ICE, Eurex
- **Market Size**: ~$15-20 billion annually
- **Participants**: Energy companies, insurers, agricultural firms, hedge funds
- **Growth Rate**: 10-15% annually
- **Accessibility**: Primarily institutional, limited retail access

### 🏛️ MAJOR EXCHANGES

**CME Group (Chicago Mercantile Exchange):**
- **Products**: Temperature futures, precipitation futures, hurricane futures
- **Locations**: US cities (major metropolitan areas)
- **Contract Size**: $20 per degree day
- **Trading Hours**: Electronic (CME Globex)
- **Margin Requirements**: ~$2,000-5,000 per contract
- **Data Fees**: $100-500/month for real-time data

**ICE (Intercontinental Exchange):**
- **Products**: Weather index futures, catastrophe futures
- **Locations**: European and US cities
- **Contract Size**: Varies by product
- **Trading Hours**: Electronic
- **Margin Requirements**: ~$1,500-4,000 per contract
- **Data Fees**: $200-600/month

**Eurex:**
- **Products**: European weather derivatives
- **Locations**: Major European cities
- **Contract Size**: €5,000 per index point
- **Trading Hours**: Electronic
- **Margin Requirements**: ~€2,000-6,000 per contract
- **Data Fees**: €150-400/month

- **Net Worth**: $1,000,000+ (many brokers)
- **Experience**: 2+ years trading experience
- **Professional Status**: Accredited investor or professional trader
- **Compliance**: Regulatory registration (NFA, CFTC)

**Brokerage Requirements:**
- **Account Type**: Professional/Institutional

**Regulatory Requirements:**

### 🌍 PRODUCT SPECIFICS

**Temperature Futures (CME):**
- **Underlying**: HDD/CDD indices
- **Locations**: 15+ US cities
- **Contract Size**: $20 per degree day
- **Settlement**: Cash based on temperature data
- **Expiry**: Monthly contracts
- **Tick Size**: 1 degree day = $20

**Precipitation Futures (CME):**
- **Underlying**: Rainfall/snowfall indices
- **Locations**: Major agricultural areas
- **Contract Size**: $100 per inch of precipitation
- **Settlement**: Cash based on NOAA data
- **Expiry**: Monthly contracts
- **Tick Size**: 0.01 inch = $1

**Hurricane Futures (CME):**
- **Underlying**: Hurricane index
- **Locations**: Gulf Coast, East Coast
- **Contract Size**: $1,000 per index point
- **Settlement**: Cash based on hurricane activity
- **Expiry**: Seasonal contracts
- **Tick Size**: 0.01 index point = $10

### 📈 TRADING STRATEGIES

**Common Strategies:**
- **Hedging**: Energy companies hedge temperature risk
- **Speculation**: Bet on weather patterns
- **Spread Trading**: Location or time spreads
- **Arbitrage**: Weather vs energy markets
- **Options**: Weather options for limited risk

**Market Participants:**
- **Energy Companies**: Natural gas, electricity hedging
- **Insurers**: Catastrophe risk management
- **Agricultural**: Crop weather protection
- **Hedge Funds**: Speculative trading
- **Utilities**: Demand hedging

### 🎯 MARKET ACCESS PATHWAYS

- **Process**: Exchange membership, clearing arrangements
- **Cost**: $10,000-50,000 setup + monthly fees
- **Timeline**: 3-6 months for approval

**Brokerage Access:**
- **Requirements**: Lower capital, professional status
- **Process**: Account opening, compliance checks

**Proprietary Trading Firms:**
- **Requirements**: Trading experience, capital contribution

- **Cost**: $5,000-25,000 capital contribution
- **Timeline**: 2-4 months for approval

### 📊 RISK FACTORS

**Market Risks:**
- **Liquidity Risk**: Limited market depth
- **Execution Risk**: Slippage, partial fills
- **Model Risk**: Weather model inaccuracies
- **Basis Risk**: Location vs contract differences

**Operational Risks:**
- **System Risk**: Trading platform failures
- **Data Risk**: Weather data errors
- **Settlement Risk**: Cash settlement issues
- **Regulatory Risk**: Rule changes

**Financial Risks:**
- **Margin Calls**: Additional capital requirements
- **Leverage Risk**: Amplified losses
- **Concentration Risk**: Weather correlation
